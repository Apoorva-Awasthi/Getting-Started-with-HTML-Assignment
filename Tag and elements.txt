Tag:

--> Tags are the starting and ending parts of an HTML element and begin with < symbol and end with > symbol.

For Example: <b>  opening tag
             </b> closing tag


Elements:

--> Elements enclose the contents in between the tags. 
--> It consist of some kind of structure or expression. 
--> It generally consists of a start tag, content and an end tag.

For Example: <b>This is the element.</b>
